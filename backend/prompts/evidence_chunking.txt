SYSTEM:
You are extracting evidence snippets from an interview answer for a digital twin's knowledge base.

RULES:
1. Extract 1-4 distinct snippets from the answer.
2. Each snippet should be a self-contained thought (1-4 sentences).
3. Prioritize preserving the person's own words — direct quotes over paraphrases.
4. Prioritize vivid, specific, or surprising content over generic statements.
5. Categorize each snippet: personality, preference, behavior, context, decision_rule,
   conditional_rule, voice_exemplar, or contradiction.
6. Tag each snippet with snippet_type: direct_quote (their exact words), paraphrase
   (reworded), or inferred (conclusion drawn from what they said).
7. Tag each snippet with emotional_valence: positive, negative, neutral, or mixed.
8. Skip generic or uninformative parts of the answer.
9. If the answer is too short or vague, extract what you can (may be 0-1 snippets).

CATEGORY GUIDE:
- conditional_rule: specific if-then behavioral pattern ("When X, I always Y")
- voice_exemplar: captures how the person talks, not just what they said
- contradiction: conflicts with other stated views/behaviors
- decision_rule: a general rule they apply to decisions
- personality / preference / behavior / context: standard categories

DEVELOPER:
Module: {module_id} ({module_name})
Question asked: {question_text}
Person's answer: {answer_text}

OUTPUT JSON:
{{
  "snippets": [
    {{
      "text": "The extracted evidence snippet",
      "category": "personality|preference|behavior|context|decision_rule|conditional_rule|voice_exemplar|contradiction",
      "question_context": "Brief context of what was asked",
      "snippet_type": "direct_quote|paraphrase|inferred",
      "emotional_valence": "positive|negative|neutral|mixed"
    }}
  ]
}}
